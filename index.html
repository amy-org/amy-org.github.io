What is Tarot?

Tarot is a deck of 78 symbolic cards used for deep self-reflection and inner guidance. Created in 1909, the Rider-Waite-Smith deck remains the most loved because every card tells a story through rich imagery.

It is not about predicting fate — it is about understanding yourself and your path with clarity and wisdom.

The Power of Intuition

Before looking up any meaning, spend time with the card’s image. Notice what feelings, colors, or stories arise naturally.

Intuition is your inner voice. The cards are designed to awaken it. Traditional meanings give you structure, but your intuition gives the message personal truth.

Simple Daily Practice

1. Draw one card
2. Sit quietly with the image for one minute
3. Write down the first feeling or thought that comes
4. Only then read the traditional meaning
